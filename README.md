# 🛒 Blinkit Sales & Inventory Dashboard
## 📌 Project Overview

The **Blinkit Sales & Inventory Dashboard** is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution built using **Power BI**. It provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, inventory management, customer ratings, seller distribution, and delivery efficiency through interactive visualizations.

The dashboard is designed to help business stakeholders monitor KPIs, identify sales trends, analyze inventory levels, and make data-driven decisions.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Blinkit operates across multiple cities and product categories, making it challenging to monitor sales, inventory, delivery performance, and customer satisfaction simultaneously.

This dashboard centralizes business metrics into a single interactive report, enabling management to quickly identify trends, monitor operational efficiency, and optimize inventory decisions.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall business performance
- Monitor inventory availability
- Compare sold quantity vs stock quantity
- Evaluate delivery performance
- Analyze seller distribution
- Track customer satisfaction
- Identify top-performing brands
- Support strategic decision-making
